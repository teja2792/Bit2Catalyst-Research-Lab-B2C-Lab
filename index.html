Research

🔬 Lab Description Bit2Catalyst Research Lab (B2C Lab) is a next-generation research hub focused on transforming digital intelligence into real-world materials innovation. The lab operates at the intersection of AI, catalysis, and advanced materials, accelerating discovery in functional nanomaterials, critical mineral extraction, and sustainable water treatment. By integrating LLMs, vision systems, and electrochemical platforms, B2C Lab pioneers a “bits → catalysts → atoms” pipeline to engineer scalable, high-impact solutions for energy and sustainability. 👨‍🔬 Founder Profile Dr. Ravi Teja A.T., PhD, is a multidisciplinary researcher bridging AI, data science, and materials engineering. His work focuses on leveraging machine learning and intelligent systems to revolutionize catalysis and materials discovery, positioning him at the forefront of AI-driven scientific innovation
