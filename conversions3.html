Butler-Volmer Equation

Relates electrode current density to overpotential at an electrochemical interface.

j = j₀ · [ exp(αFη/RT)       − exp(−(1−α)Fη/RT) ]
F = 96485 C/mol  |  R = 8.314 J/mol·K
Limiting cases
Low η: j ≈ j₀Fη/RT Anodic Tafel: j ≈ j₀exp(αFη/RT) Cathodic Tafel: j ≈ −j₀exp(−(1−α)Fη/RT)
